About University of Makeni

The University of Makeni (UNIMAK) is a leading private university in Sierra Leone, located in the city of Makeni. The institution is committed to fostering technological innovation and offers a range of programs that prepare students for the ever-evolving tech industry.

City: Makeni

Type: Private University

Available Tech Courses

  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science
  • Information Systems
  • Cybersecurity

Facilities & Features

  • State-of-the-art computer labs with the latest hardware and software
  • Tech-focused research centers
  • Expert faculty with industry knowledge
  • Innovation hubs to foster startups and entrepreneurship

Why Choose University of Makeni?

University of Makeni combines academic excellence with practical experience. With a focus on research and real-world application, students are equipped to be problem-solvers in various sectors of the technology field.
